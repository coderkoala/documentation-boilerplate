Harley Davidson Social Media Platform

Introduction

Harley-Davidson (H-D) currently runs on Kick Apps for all their social media needs and this report explores possible alternate platforms. Kick Apps is no longer actively developed and is failing to meet the various H-D requirements.

The immediate goal is to find a better social media platform for the ride planner project and to eventually adopt the platform accross all H-D campaigns.

Requirements

H-D's specific requirements for their next social media platform include (but not limited to):

  1. An API or robust solution to integrate the login with Harley profile (H-D has their own preferred customer registration)
  2. The ability to have tiered memberships based on Harley profile status
  3. Needs complete media support including: blog, status, photos and video
  4. If the solution is SaaS, it must support custom domains

    Recommendations

    Based on the above research, it is recommended that a developer spends additional time to evaluate Ning, BuddyPress and Elgg. A complete list of requirements in a BRD format would help facilitate the developer's evaluation on whether any of these platforms are viable for H-D or not.

    In addition, a high level discussion should be had as to why H-D wants to re-invent the wheel with their own social network. Facebook pages provides a robust social network platform for brands with the possibility of adding in custom FB apps for unique campaigns. With the largest user base among any social networking sites, the H-D strategy team should evaluate integrating all their campaigns to within Facebook instead of building their own and encouraging customer participation through that platform.
